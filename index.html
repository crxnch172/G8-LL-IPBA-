Famous dishes that we teach to cook

Barbeque

Savor our juicy, tender barbecue, marinated with a blend of healthy seasonings, spices, and herbs. Slowly grilled to enhance its sweet, savory flavor while keeping it soft, tender, and wholesome with every bite.

Chicken Inasal

Chicken Inasal is marinated with healthy seasonings like calamansi, black pepper, and natural herbs, then grilled to perfection for that irresistible smokey flavor. Each bite is tender, juicy, and crispy, delivering a healthy yet satisfying experience.

Liempo

Tender and flavorful, our Liempo is marinated with a mix of calamansi, garlic, black pepper, and natural spices for a perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Grilled to perfection, it delivers a smokey, savory sensation in every bite. Enjoy it with rice or as a side dish!

Steak

Juicy, smokey, and unforgettable. Our premium slices of beef are expertly smoked to perfection, delivering a rich, savory flavor in every bite. As one of our main courses, it pairs perfectly with your choice of sides. A taste to remember, an experience to savor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Smokey sarap is a fundraising project that teaches you how to cook famous and known filipino dishes like barbeque, steak, liempo, chicken inasal, and more. We focus on offering you easy and simple tutorials by using seasonings and pure and nutritious ingredients to ensure that even Sitio Bebe can enjoy the dishes we teach you to create!

The funds that our channel generates from our tutorials and cooking videos will benefit directly towards the people of Sitio Bebe, which can provide them essential healthcare resources, medical supplies, and support for respiratory health issues in the community.

Easy-to-follow cooking tutorials featuring the same dishes at Smokey Sarap. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, our videos will guide you through the preparation of your favorite Filipino meals while supporting a good cause.

Yes, we will provide regular updates on our website and social media to show how the funds are making a positive impact in Sitio Bebe.
